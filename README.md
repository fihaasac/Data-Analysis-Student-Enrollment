# Student Enrollment & Revenue Analytics Dashboard 📊

## 📌 Project Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze the enrollment trends, demographic distribution, and revenue generation of an educational institute. By transforming raw student data into an interactive dashboard, this project identifies actionable business insights to optimize marketing strategies and course offerings.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack Used
* **Tool:** Microsoft Power BI
* **Skills:** Data Visualization, Data Modeling, Business Intelligence (BI), Analytical Thinking

## 💡 Key Business Insights Discovered
1. **Revenue Drivers:** Multimedia courses (specifically DaVinci Resolve and Adobe After Effects) yield the highest financial return, indicating a strong market demand for creative tech skills.
2. **Seasonality Trends:** Enrollments experience significant spikes during April and September. **Recommendation:** Allocate the highest marketing and promotional budgets 4-6 weeks prior to these peak months.
3. **Target Demographics:** Over 52% of the active student base falls within the 20-30 age bracket. **Recommendation:** Tailor advertising copy and platforms to target young professionals seeking career upskilling.
